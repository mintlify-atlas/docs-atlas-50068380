Dolibarr ERP & CRM is an open-source web application that brings together everything your business needs — from managing customers and suppliers to handling invoices, inventory, payroll, projects, and accounting — in a single, modular platform. Built in PHP and designed for businesses of any size, Dolibarr lets you activate only the modules you need, keeping things simple while scaling as you grow.

## Quick start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Dolibarr">
    Download the latest release and point your web server to the `htdocs/` directory, or pull the Docker image for a one-command setup. See [Installation](/installation) for full details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run the setup wizard">
    Navigate to `/install/` in your browser and follow the on-screen wizard to configure your database and create the first admin account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate modules">
    Go to **Home → Setup → Modules** and turn on the features your business needs — CRM, invoicing, stock, accounting, and more. Everything else stays out of the way.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import your data">
    Use the built-in import tools to load existing customers, products, and suppliers, or start fresh and let your team populate data as they work.
  </Step>
</Steps>
